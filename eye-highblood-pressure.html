High Blood Pressure Eye Exams

Diagnosis & Treatment of Hypertension Retinopathy

High blood pressure can really take its toll on your body. It’s not just your heart that is at risk when you are struggling with hypertension. Your eyes may also be at risk. Hypertension retinopathy is a serious condition that you need to watch out for, so regular exams from a qualified eye doctor are an important part of protecting yourself from this condition. Sutphin Eye Care offers comprehensive care and monitoring for this condition to help ensure that your eyes are well protected. Book an Appointment now at Sutphin Eye Care for your entire family.

Understanding Hypertensive Retinopathy

Hypertensive retinopathy happens when a patient with high blood pressure goes untreated for a long period of time. The continued high blood pressure can cause damage to the retina, which can impact the ability to see. Anyone with uncontrolled hypertension can develop this condition, but the risk increases the longer an individual has high blood pressure problems. In addition, age increases the risk of high blood pressure, which in turn increases the risk of hypertensive retinopathy.
