Popular Frames to Consider when Choosing Your New Eyeglasses

Eyeglass frames are available in a variety of materials that offer their own unique advantages depending on the situation..

Metal Frames

Metal is the most popular frame material. Popular metals include:

  • Beta titanium – This titanium and aluminum alloy makes frames more flexible than titanium alone.

  • Memory metal- This titanium and nickel alloy makes frames incredibly flexible. It’s a great choice for athletes, kids, or anyone who puts a lot of stress on their frames.

  • Beryllium - This lightweight, affordable metal is strong and flexible while also resisting corrosion or tarnishing. This is a good choice for those who spend time near salt water or who have high skin acidity.

  • Stainless steel - Stainless steel is flexible but also strong and resistant to corrosion. These frames are available in different colors in both matte and polished finishes.

Plastic Frames

  • Zyl – This is the most popular kind of plastic frame and is affordable and lightweight. It’s available in many colors, including color layering.

  • Propionate - This nylon-based frame is known for its durability and flexibility while also being hypoallergenic. It’s a great choice for athletes.
