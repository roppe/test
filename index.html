Glaucoma Testing

Glaucoma refers to the eye disease that damages the optic nerve. This can occur for a number of reasons, but typically fluid buildup in the front of the eye is the cause. This fluid buildup creates pressure that damages the optic nerve. Because this damage cannot be reversed, it is critical to get an early diagnosis.

Diabetic Eye Exams

A diabetic eye exam is an eye exam designed to detect diabetic eye problems, including diabetic retinopathy. This exam involves dilation of the eyes, which gives the doctor the ability to see the retina and examine them for any signs of retinopathy.
