Eye diseases can happen to anyone, regardless of age, some are more common among older patients. These include:

  • Glaucoma When the eye fluid doesn’t drain properly and pressure builds up, it causes nerve damage to the optic nerve, which can lead to blindness..

Comprehensive Services for Senior Eye Care

When you come to Sutphin Eye Doctor for your senior eye care needs, our caring optometrists will take the time to perform a comprehensive eye exam for seniors, looking for signs of these and other conditions. This exam goes beyond simply checking your prescription. Some of the tests included in this exam are:

  • Refractive error assessment
  • Eye muscle capability test
  • Slit lamp exam of the back of the eyes
  • Intraocular pressure test for glaucoma

If we find evidence of any problems, Sutphin has the latest treatment options to help protect your vision. From laser treatments and surgeries to eye drops and vision therapies, we will help you do what you need to protect your vision and stop the progression of eye disease. If we do not find evidence of a problem, we will help you take the right home care measures to protect your vision for the future.
