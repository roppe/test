Signs of Vision Problems in Children

For many parents, spotting signs of eye concerns in their kids is not easy. Children are excellent at compensating for vision problems, and signs in toddlers and preschoolers are often hard to see. Some common early symptoms of vision problems include: Poor fine motor skills,Inability to do simple puzzles Inability to focus on pictures in a book,Frequent headaches,Rubbing the eyes,Sensitivity to light, Squinting,Tilting the head Crossing eyes. Most common cases of eyes problems in children:

  • Refractive error
  • Astigmatism
  • Near sightedness
  • Far sightedness
  • Amblyopia ( Lazy Eye )
  • Poor coordination between eyes
  • Poor hand and eye coordination
