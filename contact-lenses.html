What Happens During a Contact Lens Exam?

Why should you choose Sutphin Eye Doctor for your contact lenses needs? When you do, you will enjoy several key factors that set us apart from other family eye care clinics.

A contact lens exam begins with a comprehensive eye exam that includes:

  • Visual acuity tests using a projected eye chart to assess the clarity of your near and far vision

  • Eye movement tests to see how rapidly and easily your eyes track to different points

  • Cover tests to assess the individual strength of each eye

  • Depth perception checks to see if your eyes move in tandem

  • Tests designed to determine the health of corneas, irises, eyelids, and more
