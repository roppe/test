Why To Choose Sutphin Eye Care Center

At Sutphin Eye Care our priority is to provide a comprehensive and high quality eye care for our patients. The examination that is conducted by our experienced eye doctor entails determining the best prescription and corrective lenses for our optimum vision as well as detailed medical examination of your eyes to detect and prevent a variety of diseases such as cataract, glaucoma, macular degeneration and other eye diseases.

Further, our experienced stuff will guide you in choosing from our exceptional quality frames that will be customized to meet your visual need based on the doctor’s recommendation.Our staff is always updated with the latest technological advances to meet your vision needs such as transitions, progressives and anti-reflective lenses. Meet Our Practice Team

Dr. Farshad Haiimpour

Dr. Farshad Haiimpour is a graduate of State University of New York School of Optometry, 1999. He has extensive training in diagnosis, management and treatment of conditions of the eye and the visual system. He is licensed to treat glaucoma, allergic conditions, infections, in addition to co-management of laser vision correction with distinguished surgeons in the New York area.

Dr. Haiimpour is a contact lens specialist who can custom fit irregular shaped eyes with RGP, bifocal, and prosthetic contact lenses and can prescribe contact lenses to those patients who can not wear conventional contact lenses for any medical reasons.

Dr. Haiimpour has been in private practice since March 2000, and is highly experienced in prescribing and dispensing prescription glasses and contact lenses for children and adults.
