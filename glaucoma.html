Glaucoma Testing & Screening

Glaucoma is an eye disease that damages the optic nerve. This can occur for a number of reasons, but the typical cause is a buildup of fluid in the front of the eye. This fluid buildup creates pressure that damages the optic nerve. Because this damage cannot be reversed, it is critical to get an early diagnosis. Sutphin Eye Care can help with our glaucoma testing and treatments options.

How Often Should I Be Screened for Glaucoma?

At Sutphin Eye Care, we screen all adult patients for glaucoma, and we recommend eye exams every year. However, if you are at high risk for glaucoma or are over the age of 60, we recommend more frequent screenings to protect your vision.

Why Is Early Glaucoma Detection Important?

Glaucoma is sometimes called “the silent thief of sight” because it is asymptomatic in the early stages, yet can eventually cause irreversible vision loss. To determine whether you have glaucoma or other eye diseases, consult our experienced eye doctor for a comprehensive eye exam.

Glaucoma Testing in Queens,NY

When glaucoma is found early, the treatment is fairly simple. Prescription glaucoma eye drops can help preserve healthy pressure inside the eye and prevent the risk of vision loss. When used daily, glaucoma eye drops help reduce the amount of fluid in the eye. Eye drops are the cheapest of all glaucoma treatments, so this is one of the first recommendations your eye doctor will make.
