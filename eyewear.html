Choose Sutphin Eye Doctor for Eyeglasses

Your vision is what matters most at Sutphin Eye Doctor. Our optometrists have years of experience providing comprehensive eye care individually suited to your unique needs. From performing pediatric exams, choosing the perfect frames and lenses, or treating an eye disorder, our goal is to put you on the path to crystal clear vision.

Wearing the perfect pair of glasses first requires a comprehensive eye exam to determine your unique vision needs. An eye exam at Sutphin Eye Doctor includes:

  • Visual Acuity Test We use a projected eye chart to evaluate how clearly you see images using your near vision.

  • Cover Test We evaluate the strength of each eye by having you cover one eye while reading a vision chart.

  • Ocular Motility Analysis - We gauge how quickly and smoothly your eyes move.

  • Stereopsis Test We ensure your depth perception is normal and that your eyes coordinate with one another.

  • Slit Lamp Exam UOur optometrists use a special microscope to evaluate the health of your eyelids, corneas, irises, and more to spot signs of eye disease.

  • Pupil Dilation After inserting special eye drops to enlarge your pupils, our optometrists can more easily detect eye abnormalities.

These are just some examples of what may occur with a comprehensive eye exam. As we mentioned before—each set of eyes is different, so your optometrist will decide what’s right for you. Don't wait contact Sutphin Eye Care .Dr. Farshad now to Book your Appointment.

Getting the perfect pair of glasses isn’t a one-size fits all approach. After your exam, our optometrists and staff ensure you walk out of our office wearing the perfect pair of eyeglasses guaranteed to give you clear vision. We specialize in:

Eyeglasses – We take into consideration your lens prescription, age and activity level, and the style of frames you desire to create the best pair of eyeglasses for your needs.
Eyeglass Lenses – Whether your prescription calls for single vision lenses, progressive lenses, or polycarbonate lenses, we’ll guide you in choosing the best lenses for you.
Sunglasses – Prescription sunglasses are a great choice to help protect your eyes from damaging UV light that can cause vision problems like cataracts and retinal degeneration in later years. Our sunglasses block 100 percent of UV light so you can feel safe that your eyes are being protected.
Anti-Reflective Lenses – These special lenses help you see more clearly by passing more light into your eyes and reducing frustrating glare and reflections caused by light rebounding off your lenses.
